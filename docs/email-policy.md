# Email & Anti-Spam Policy (ChimeCheck)

Effective date: **[INSERT DATE]**

ChimeCheck sends **transactional emails only** to support operation of the service (for example: verification, password resets, caregiver invitations, check-in confirmations if enabled, missed check-in alerts, billing receipts if applicable).

## 1) No marketing email (current policy)
We do not send marketing newsletters or promotional emails at this time.

If we introduce marketing emails in the future, we will:
- Obtain opt-in where required
- Provide clear unsubscribe mechanisms
- Include required sender identification and address information
- Maintain suppression lists and honor opt-out requests

## 2) Who receives emails
Emails are sent only to:
- Users of the Service
- Caregivers explicitly added by a user, for the purpose of receiving service alerts

## 3) Prohibited use
The Service may not be used to:
- Send unsolicited messages (“spam”)
- Send deceptive or misleading content
- Harvest addresses or circumvent suppression lists
- Send content that violates applicable law or third-party policies

## 4) Contact
If you believe you received an email in error, contact:
- **chimecheck@onetapstudio.app**
